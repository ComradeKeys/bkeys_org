About Brigham

TLDR

The hat I normally wear is that I am a Software Developer, there are many aspects about technology that interest me greatly which can make it hard to put it down. I am a supporter and Associate Member of the Free Software Foundation. Alot of this page is about the other interests that I hold outside of developing software, and can be treated as somewhat of an unrelated section to this website.

Astronomy

A fascinating subject. I enjoy to follow various space projects and seeingwhere they lead can be a pretty awesome experience for me. I also believe that a strong space program tends to being out the best of any nation. My favorite space projects currently are the Kwangmyŏngsŏng-3, Rosetta, and the Chinese Lunar Base. My interest in astronomy has also inspired my creation of Observable, an android app that allows you to see a 3D view of a given celestial body.

Geometry

I feel that this often discounted as a subject that is not particularly interesting. For example, you cannot walk up to someone and mention the Platonic solids, and spark a discussion. This would likely work on me however. The math behind many of these solids is interesting. I feel like they have a beauty to them. The ancient greek philosopher Plato believed that the classical elements were shaped like the solids named after him. Which leads to my next interest, which is philosophy.

Philosophy

I find learning about subjects outside of software development make me better at writing software. The writings of Plato interest me greatly, inparticular the philosophy of Socrates. My favorite dialogues are: Apology, Crito, and Phaedo. The concepts discussed in these dialogues are simply rivoting.

Aviation

There are many aspects of aviation that are interesting. I also think that planes inparticular are generally aesthetically pleasing, however this feeling does not extend to helicopters. My favorite aircraft of all time is the Mig-15, what an aesthetically pleasing aircraft! I also enjoy greatly, the hobby of building and flying RC planes.
